SERVICES


Daily Exterior Damp Wash:

The exterior of your car is washed using Automotive Grade dry/damp wash products. We ensure that highest quality products to give your car the showroom shine. The products also have carnauba wax, which give a glossy look while also serving as a protective layer on the paint.

Weekly Exterior Exhaustive Wash:

During our weekly exhaustive wash, we do a complete wash which includes Tyre Dressing, Rim Shine, MudGuard Scrub and Underbody Pressure Wash in addition to the service of Daily Exterior Wash.

Bi Weekly Interior Clean:

The interiors of the car are cleaned Bi Weekly, during which we vacuum the interiors, shine the dashboard, clean the upholstery, wash the rubber mats and clean air vents. You also get a complimentary Air Freshener which keeps your car fresh for the coming weeks.
